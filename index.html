WELCOME TO THE MACROMEDIA MASTER PROGRAM

Learn the rules of the game! With an understanding of the macro fundamental landscape, we can assess the unfolding reality and subsequently the implications of the unfolding trends. Macro fundamentals provide the foundation upon which we can use traditional fundamental and technical analysis to support our outlook. The end result is a much more holistic approach to investing.
